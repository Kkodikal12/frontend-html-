Sangam

Matrimonial for Indian language speakers

Where language, values, and families come together

Sangam means “confluence.” Our mission is to bring kind, compatible people together across India’s rich tapestry of languages—Konkani, Tamil, Hindi, and many more—while honoring culture, consent, and safety.

We believe great matches start with respect. That’s why Sangam prioritizes verified profiles (your real face photo), thoughtful discovery by language and region, and privacy by design. You’re in control of what you share, with whom, and when.

How Sangam works (quick peek)

  1. Create your profile with name, age, city/state/country, languages you speak (multi-select), and a clear face photo.
  2. Use filters to discover people who share your language(s) and region.
  3. Control your visibility and connections. Report or block in one tap.

Security, Privacy & Account

We’re serious about trust and safety.

  • Report & block: easy tools to keep your experience respectful.
  • Data requests: export or delete your data any time.
